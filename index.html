Your Smile, Our Passion!

Welcome to Fishikta Dental Clinic, where your oral health takes center stage. Our dental clinic is dedicated to providing exceptional care, ensuring that every smile receives the attention it deserves.

Dental care services encompass a broad range of treatments aimed at maintaining oral health, preventing dental issues, and addressing specific concerns.

Dental Examinations

Routine check-ups and examinations to assess overall oral health.

Dental Cleanings

Professional cleaning of teeth to remove plaque and tartar, preventing gum disease and cavities.

Digital X-rays

Diagnostic imaging for a detailed view of teeth, gums, and jawbone.

Fillings

Restoration of decayed teeth with materials like composite resin or amalgam.

Crowns and Bridges

Replacement of missing teeth by bridging the gap between adjacent teeth.

Dental Implants

Surgical placement of artificial tooth roots to support crowns or dentures.

Orthodontics

Treatment to correct the alignment of teeth and jaws, including braces and clear aligners.

Extractions

Removal of damaged or problematic teeth.

Root Canal Therapy

Treatment to remove infected or damaged pulp from a tooth's root canal.

Teeth Whitening

Cosmetic procedures to lighten the color of teeth for a brighter smile.
